Why StudioDrop exists

Built by a reseller who got tired of subscriptions. Monthly fees, upload caps, credit packs that run out mid-listing-day — product photography tools treat volume sellers as a revenue stream to squeeze.

StudioDrop is the opposite: a one-time purchase that runs entirely on your own Mac using Apple's built-in machine learning. It's the tool I wanted for my own listings — fast enough for five hundred photos, private enough that nothing ever leaves my machine, and paid for exactly once.

Questions

FAQ

Do my photos get uploaded anywhere?

No. All processing happens on your Mac using Apple's Vision framework. The app makes no network connections at all — it works fully offline.

Is it really a one-time purchase?

Yes. Buy it once on the Mac App Store and it's yours — unlimited photos, no credits, no renewal, and free updates.

What if a cutout isn't perfect?

Results depend on the source photo. The app includes a built-in tips guide; the big three are even lighting, an uncluttered backdrop, and strong contrast between your item and the background.

What does it need to run?

macOS 12 or later on an Apple Silicon Mac (M1 and newer).
