Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version: StudioDrop processes your photos entirely on your device, and we collect nothing.

What we collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • Your photos never leave your Mac. The app makes no network connections.
  • There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services of any kind.

What stays on your Mac

Settings — such as your chosen output folder, background colour and aspect ratio — are stored locally on your device and nowhere else.

Support emails

If you contact support, we use your message solely to help you. It is never shared, sold, or added to any mailing list.
