How do I use StudioDrop?

Drag a folder of product photos into the window (or click browse), choose where to save, pick a background and shape, and click Process. Finished photos appear in your chosen folder; your originals are never touched.

Some cutouts aren't perfect — why?

Results depend on the source photo. Click the ? button in the app's title bar for the full photo-tips guide. The three biggest wins: soft even lighting, a clean uncluttered backdrop, and strong contrast between your item and the background.

Where do my photos go?

Nowhere. Everything is processed on your Mac. The app never connects to the internet — it works fully offline.

What formats are supported?

In: HEIC (straight from iPhone), JPG, PNG, WebP, TIFF and BMP. Out: JPG for solid-colour backgrounds, PNG when you choose a transparent background.

What do the aspect ratio options mean?

1:1 is the square standard most marketplaces recommend. The portrait, tall, vertical and wide options match social feeds, pin boards, stories and web banners. The longest edge is always 2000 pixels, so quality never drops.
